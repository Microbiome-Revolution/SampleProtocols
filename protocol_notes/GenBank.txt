Any assembled DNA sequences should be submitted to Genbank to be assigned an accession number.

Genbank is the NIH genetic sequence database, an annotated collection of all publicly available DNA sequences.

Genbank Homepage - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/genbank/

How to Submit Data to Genbank - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/genbank/submit/

Genbank Submission Types - https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/genbank/submit_types/
